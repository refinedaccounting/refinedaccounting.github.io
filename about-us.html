Background & Interests

Barry Moore is a Certified Practicing Accountant (CPA) and graduated from Curtin University with a Bachelor of Commerce. Prior to completing his degree, Barry worked for many years in the commercial industry of plumbing. As a manager, he was involved in the supply of large commercial developments in the Perth CBD.

After a life changing decision, Barry transitioned from the construction industry to a career working with numbers, heading towards a role as a practicing accountant. His background enables him to liase with customers in business-related terms about their business.

Barry's hobbies include playing football, travelling, cycling and being a taxi driver for his children.
