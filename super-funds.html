Super Funds

When it comes to retirement assets, it's not easy to know “how much is enough”. As our life expectancy increases for both men and women, the bad news is that without adequate superannuation, retirement income lifestyle could be less than desired.

Refined Accounting has the opinion that it's not just those close to retirement who need to think about their super. More and more it seems that saving for retirement should be a lifelong objective.

Specialising in Self Managed Superannuation Funds, we can provide a variety of services including:

  • Self Managed Superannuation Fund Establishment
  • SMSF Consultancy & Strategic Advice
  • Meeting the many Compliance (Accounting, Taxation & Audit) Obligations
  • Transition to Retirement Strategies
  • Pension Income Streams.

You concentrate on the main purpose of your Self Managed Superannuation Fund creating the wealth, and let Refined Accounting worry about the detail!
